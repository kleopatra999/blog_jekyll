--- layout: post title: 'HOWTO: Search Pidgin''s logs for a regular expression' date: '2012-12-13T14:57:00.000-08:00' author: Russell Dickenson tags: - linux - tip - howto modified_time: '2012-12-13T14:57:25.675-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-510053790932706340.post-1460258176531910110 blogger_orig_url: http://phayzshift.blogspot.com/2012/12/howto-search-pidgins-logs-for-regular.html --- I wanted to search all of Pidgin's logs for mention of a subject I remembered was discussed a few months ago.

From within the directory for Pidgin's log files - ~/.purple - I tried the following command:

grep -R <regex> *.txt"

Sadly this failed miserably so I asked for help. Immediately someone came up with the answer:

grep -R <regex> **/*.txt
